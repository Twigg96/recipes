No Bake Cookies

Ingredients:

2 Cups of Sugar
½ Cups of Milk
¼ Cup of Cocoa Powder
½ Cup of Butter
1 Teaspoon of Vanilla
½ Cup of Peanut Butter
¼ Teaspoon of Salt
3 cups of Oatmeal

Step 1:
Combine the sugar, milk, cocoa and butter in a pan and bring to a boil over medium heat for 1 minute
Step 2:
Remove from heat and add in peanut butter until combined
Step 3:
Stir in vanilla and then oatmeal
Step 4:
Use a Tablespoon to form a ball and place on parchment paper
Step 5:
When cooled you can roll in powdered sugar

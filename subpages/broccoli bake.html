Broccoli Bake

Ingredients:

2 10oz bags of frozen broccoli
1 Box of stuffing
1 10oz can of Cream of Mushroom soup
1 Cup of mayonnaise
½ Cup of finely chopped onion
1 Cup of shredded cheddar cheese
¼ Cup of melted butter

Step 1:
Mix Cream of Mushroom, mayonnaise,onions, and cheese in bowl
Step 2:
Place broccoli in a baking dish
Step 3:
Top with the stuffing
Step 4:
Top with the stuffing
Step 5:
Pour the melted butter over the top of the stuffing
Step 6:
Place the bowl mixture on top and mix together
Step 7:
Bake at 350° for 35-40 minutes

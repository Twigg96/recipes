Lasagna/Baked Ziti

I put these 2 together because they basically use the same ingredients. The only difference is the noodles, and, well, the lasagna is layered and the ziti is all mixed together.

Ingredients:

1 small container ricotta cheese
Mozzarella cheese (enough to fill the empty ricotta container)
¼ cup parmesan cheese (I normally just use the top to measure)
1 egg
1 Tablespoon of Italian Seasoning
1 lb. ground beef (you can use Italian sausage)
Homemade sauce (okay ...you can use store bought, I would buy 2 jars to make sure)

Step 1:
Cook meat
Step 2:
Cook noodles
Step 3:
Mix together cheeses, egg and seasoning
Step 3:
Lasagna-layer ingredients or ziti- mix well
Step 4:
Can top with additional mozzarella cheese
Step 5:
Preheat oven 375°
Step 6:
Cover with aluminum foil
Step 7:
Bake ziti until bubbly or bake lasagna 30-40 minutes

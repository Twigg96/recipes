Pizza Dough

Ingredients:

4 Cups of Flour
1 pkg or 2¼ Teaspoons of Instant Yeast
1 Tablespoon of Sugar
1 Teaspoon of Salt
2 Tablespoons of Oil
1½ Cups of Warm Water

Step 1:
Combine water, sugar and yeast.Set aside for 5 minutes until it becomes foamy on top
Step 2:
In a bowl add flour and salt
Step 3:
Add oil to yeast mixture and pour into flour, mix well with a spoon
Step 4:
Put on countertop and knead for 5 minutes
Step 5:
Set aside for ½ hour to 1 hour covered in a warm place until doubled in size
Step 6:
Roll out, place on pizza sheet and bake for 5 minutes at 425°
Step 7:
Spread sauce and favorite toppings and put back in oven for 12 minutes
Step 8:
Enjoy!

Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

1 Can of Pineapple Chunks
1 Can of Crushed Pineapple
1 Can of Mandarin Oranges
1 Can of Fruit Salad Mix
1 Jar of Maraschino Cherries
1 Container of 16oz Cool Whip
1 Cup of Sour Cream
Marshmallows
Coconut Flakes

Step 1:
Drain all fruit and add to large bowl
Step 2:
Drain cherries and cut in half
Step 3:
Mix together Cool Whip and sour cream and add to the fruit
Step 4:
Put as many marshmallows and coconut flakes in as desired
Step 5:
You may add more of any of the canned fruit as you wish

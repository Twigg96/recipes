Beef & Ramen

1 lb. of Ground Beef
1 Bag of Frozen Broccoli
2 Packets of Ramen
If you want to add peppers and onions you can just saute half an onion and half a pepper (I make it both ways, with and without p & o)

Step 1:
If you use peppers and onions, cook until tender
when done gently blot some of the moisture out
Step 2:
Add ground beef
Step 3:
Pre-cook frozen broccoli in microwave
Step 3:
Cook ramen
Step 4:
Add brocolli and cooked ramen(noodles only) to ground beef
Step 5:
Top with sauce and mix

Meatloaf

Ingredients:

2 Pounds of Ground Beef
½ Cup of Bread Crumbs
¼ Parmesan cheese
1 Egg
2 Tablespoons of Milk
½ Onion (if desired)
Salt/ pepper to taste
1/3 Cup of Ketchup
2 Tablespoons of Brown Sugar
1 Tablespoon of Mustard

Step 1:
Mix first 7 ingredients together in a bowl
Step 2:
You can either place in a 9x5 pan lined with 2 slices of bread(to soak up grease) or a cookie sheet lined with aluminum foil
Step 3:
Mix the last 3 ingredients in bowl and place on top of meat mixture
Step 4:
Bake in oven at 350° for about an hour or until no longer pink in the center

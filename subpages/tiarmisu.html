Tiaramisu

Ingredients:

1 Package of 8 oz. Cream Cheese
3 Cups of Cold Milk
2 Packages of Vanilla Instant Pudding
1 8oz. Tub of Cool Whip
Â½ Cup of Strong Brewed Coffee
Grated Chocolate for decoration

Step 1:
Beat cream cheese with mixer until creamy
Step 2:
Add in milk and pudding mix and continue to beat until smooth
Step 3:
Stir in 2 cups of Cool Whip
Step 4:
Start layering your glass dish with wafers dipped in the coffee and the mixture how ever you would like
Step 5:
Top with remaining Cool Whip and chocolate

Enjoy!
